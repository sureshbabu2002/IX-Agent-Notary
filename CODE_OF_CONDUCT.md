# Code of Conduct

This project is maintained to be professional, technical, and constructive.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and keep feedback actionable.
- Keep discussions focused on engineering, security, product fit, and evidence.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Posting exploit details publicly without coordination (see `SECURITY.md`).
- Deliberate attempts to derail discussion or spam issues/PRs.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and productive.
